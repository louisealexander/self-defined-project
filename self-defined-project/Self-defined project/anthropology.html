NUA and UEA Collaboration

Anthropology of Norwich

During the final term of my second year on the illustration degree, I was tasked with a collaborative project with the anthropology course at the University of East Anglia.
We explored the repercussions of the pandemic on Norwich market, and how it's sellers had to adapt to the affects of covid on the industry.
